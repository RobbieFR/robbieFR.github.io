Soit $x \in \mathbf{R}$ tel que $\tan \left(\frac{x}{2}\right)$ et $\tan (x)$ existent. On pose $t=\tan \left(\frac{x}{2}\right) $

$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1-t^{2}}{1+t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{2 t}{1-t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1}{1+t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=1+t^{2}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{2 t}{1+t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1}{1-t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=2 t$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1+t^{2}}{2 t}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1}{t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=1-t^{2}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1+t^{2}}{1-t^{2}}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{1-t^{2}}{2 t}$
$\square \tan (x)=\frac{t}{2}$
